Discover Amazing Destinations

South India Tour

Explore South India by visiting Madurai's Meenakshi Amman Temple. In Rameswaram, see the sacred Ramanathaswamy Temple and relax on the beaches. Kanyakumari offers stunning sunrises and the Vivekananda Rock Memorial. In Thiruvananthapuram, visit the Padmanabhaswamy Temple and unwind at Kovalam Beach. Enjoy a peaceful houseboat cruise through the backwaters of Alleppey. Experience the wildlife and spice plantations in Thekkady. Finally, marvel at the tea gardens and misty hills of Munnar.

Hill Station

Sure, here is a paragraph with the given hill station destinations: Start your hill station tour in Madurai before heading to the cool climes of Kodaikanal, known for its serene lakes and lush landscapes. Continue to Coimbatore, a gateway to the Nilgiri hills, and then visit Ooty, famous for its tea gardens and botanical gardens. Explore the coffee plantations and scenic beauty of Coorg. In Mysore, enjoy the regal architecture and vibrant culture. Next, visit Bengaluru, a bustling city with beautiful parks and a pleasant climate. Conclude your tour in Karnataka and visit the sacred Tirupati Temple.

Heritage Tour

Begin your heritage tour in Madurai with the historic Meenakshi Amman Temple. Next, visit Trichy to explore the Ranganathaswamy Temple. In Thanjavur, admire the Brihadeeswarar Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Continue to Kumbakonam to see its ancient temples and architecture. Discover the temples of Chitharambam before heading to Pondicherry, known for its French colonial heritage and charming streets. Conclude your tour in Chennai with its rich history and vibrant cultural landmarks.

Dharsan Package

Embark on a spiritual journey starting in Madurai with a visit to the Meenakshi Amman Temple. Travel to Thiruvannamalai to see the sacred Arunachaleswarar Temple. In Vellore, visit the renowned Golden Temple. Proceed to Thirupati for a darshan at the famous Venkateswara Temple. In Chennai, explore various temples and religious sites. Visit Samayapuram to experience the Mariamman Temple. Continue to Thirupattur and Kanchipuram to see their historic temples and religious heritage.

Festival & Event Tours

Experience vibrant festivals and events with this package. Begin in Madurai during the Meenakshi Thirukalyanam Festival, a grand celebration at the Meenakshi Amman Temple. Move to Thiruvannamalai to witness the Deepam Festival, a significant event at the Arunachaleswarar Temple. In Vellore, enjoy the colorful celebrations of the Golden Temple festival. Visit Thirupati for the Brahmotsavam festival, a major event at the Venkateswara Temple. Explore Chennai's cultural events and festivals. Experience the lively festivities at Samayapuram, Thirupattur, and Kanchipuram, each known for its unique local celebrations and traditions.
